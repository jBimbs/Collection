Cavite State University

Mission

Cavite State University shall provide excellent, equitable and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences and technology through quality instruction and responsive research and development activities. It shall produce professional, skilled and morally upright individuals for global competitiveness.

Vision

The premier university in historic Cavite globally recognized for excellence in character development, academics, research, innovation and sustainable community engagement.

Mandate

“The University shall primarily provide advance instruction and professional training in agriculture, science and technology, education and other related fields, undertake research and extension services, and provide progressive leadership in these areas.”

Truth,Excellence and Service

To develop competent, socially conscious, and globally competitive graduates, CvSU Bacoor provides high quality education .The campus is home to a diverse community of scholars, offering a wide array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines, enabling students to pursue their academic and career aspiration. In order to promote socializing and leadership development, the university offers organizations and services to its students. It is dedicated to giving graduates the knowledge, abilities, and morals they need to succeed and make positive contributions to society.
